Who Am I

I'm Kashvi Agrawal, a B.Tech student in Robotics and Artificial Intelligence at Jaypee Institute of Information Technology with a strong passion for technology and innovation. My interests lie at the intersection of software and hardware, where I explore areas such as Web Development, IoT, Embedded Systems, Artificial Intelligence, and MATLAB. As an aspiring engineer, I am always eager to take on new challenges, explore emerging technologies, and grow through hands-on experience. This portfolio is a reflection of that journey—showcasing the projects I've built, the skills I'm developing, and the milestones I've achieved along the way.
